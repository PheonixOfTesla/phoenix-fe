Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Phoenix JARVIS collects the following types of data to provide personalized health and life optimization insights:

Wearable Device Data (via Fitbit, Polar)

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep duration, stages, and quality scores
  • Daily step count and activity levels
  • Recovery scores and readiness metrics
  • Blood oxygen levels (SpO2)
  • Resting heart rate

User-Provided Data

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, duration)
  • Nutrition data (meals, macros, calories)
  • Goals and habit tracking
  • Personal notes and preferences

Optional Integrations

  • Google Calendar events (with your permission)
  • Financial overview data (if you choose to connect)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized insights - Analyze patterns in your health and performance
  • Generate predictions - Forecast illness risk, burnout, and performance trends
  • Automate interventions - Proactively adjust workouts, schedules, and recommendations
  • Detect correlations - Identify connections between stress, spending, sleep, and performance
  • Improve the service - Enhance AI models and features

3. Data Storage and Security

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256).

Storage: Data is stored on secure MongoDB Atlas servers with automated backups.

Access Control: Only authenticated users can access their own data via JWT tokens.

Retention: Data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only share data with:

  • Service providers - Cloud hosting (Railway, Vercel), database (MongoDB Atlas) - only as necessary to operate the service
  • Wearable companies - Only to retrieve your data via authorized OAuth connections
  • Legal requirements - If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Delete your data - Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect integrations - Revoke access to Fitbit, Polar, Google, etc. at any time
  • Export your data - Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of insights - Disable specific features like predictions or interventions

6. Cookies and Tracking

Phoenix uses minimal cookies:

  • Authentication tokens - To keep you logged in (stored in localStorage)
  • No third-party tracking - We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar

7. Children's Privacy

Phoenix is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:
